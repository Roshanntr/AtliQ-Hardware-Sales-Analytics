# Overview
This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze sales, finance, marketing, supply chain, and executive performance data for AtliQ Hardware.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue, profitability, customer performance, product trends, and market performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project was to provide a centralized analytics platform that helps business leaders:
Monitor key business KPIs in real time
Identify top-performing products, customers, and markets
Analyze profitability across regions and business segments
Discover growth opportunities and operational challenges
Support data-driven strategic decision-making

# Tools & Technologies
Power BI Desktop
Power Query (ETL & Data Transformation)
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modeling
Interactive Dashboard Design

# Key Insights
Revenue Performance
Generated total revenue of ₹987M across all markets.
Achieved sales volume of 2.43 million units.
Recorded total profit of ₹24.7M.
Market Analysis
Delhi NCR emerged as the highest-performing market, generating ₹521M in revenue and contributing 52.8% of total revenue.
Mumbai ranked second with ₹150M in revenue and a 15.2% contribution.
Customer Analysis
Electricalsara Stores was identified as the top customer, contributing ₹414M in revenue.
Other major contributors included Excel Stores and Premium Stores.
Product Performance
Analysis highlighted the top-performing product categories driving overall business growth.
Identified the top 20% of products contributing approximately 80% of total sales, supporting focused business and inventory strategies.
Profitability Analysis
Bengaluru and Kanpur reported negative profitability, indicating opportunities for operational improvement.
Markets such as Surat and Bhubaneswar demonstrated strong profit margins despite lower revenue contributions, highlighting potential expansion opportunities.

# 💡 Business Recommendations
Expand High-Performing Markets
Increase investments in Delhi NCR and Mumbai.
Focus on high-value customers and high-margin product categories.
Improve Underperforming Regions
Conduct detailed root-cause analysis for markets experiencing negative profitability.
Optimize pricing, inventory, and customer acquisition strategies.
Scale High-Margin Markets
Expand operations in markets demonstrating strong profitability.
Leverage successful business models from these regions to drive sustainable growth.

# Project Outcomes
Built a scalable Power BI reporting solution for business performance monitoring.
Developed robust data models and advanced DAX calculations for KPI tracking.
Delivered actionable insights across revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance.
Enabled data-driven decision-making through interactive and executive-friendly dashboards.
